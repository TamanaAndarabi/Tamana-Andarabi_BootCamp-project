Women In Afghanistan

Afghanistan's Population is about 34 million and from this 15 million are male and 14.2 million are female. about 22% of the afghan people are urbanite and the remaining 78% live in rural areas as tradition,most womin are married soon after completing high school.Many live as housewives for the remainder of thier live.In 1992 the government under Mohammad Najibullah transitioned to the Islamic State Of Afghanistan. War in Afghanistan continued into a new phase when Gulbudden Hekmatyar started a bombardmen campagin against the Islamic state in kabule.Women began to be more restricted after Hekmatyar was integrared into the Islamic state as Afghan Prime Minister in 1996.He demainded for women who appeared on TV to be fired.Women had been kidnapped and some of them raped.Like thier leader Mullah Omar,The Taliban declared that the women were forbidden to go to work and that they were not to leave their homes.Women were denied formal education and usuallay forced to stay at home.During the Taliban's Five_Year rule,Women in Afghanistan were put under house arrest.
