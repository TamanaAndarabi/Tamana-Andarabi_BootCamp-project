Cultuer

The most women in Afghanistan work is tailoring,and a large percentage of the population are professional tailors.Since the overthrow of the Taliban government, many women have become involved in large-scale activities inside and outside the government. About 50,000 women are employed by the Afghan Ministry of Trade and Industry

For example,Meena Rahmani became the first women in Afghanistan to open a bowling center in Kaboul.Also, Hamedeh Safi is another business woman who has been producing handicrafts for almost 3 years. Ms. Safi's main job is to produce traditional and modern local clothes for men and women.
