Sport

Women's sports in Afghanistan have grown significantly compared to previous years and have achieved good results in this field. During these years, many sports complexes have been created for women in different parts of the country.For example, the sports complex called (Afghanistan Cultural and Sports Foundation), which is headed by Mohammad Afzal Azizi, the coaches of all sports in this foundation are women and girls. The head of this foundation, Mohammad Afzal Azizi, says: Because women and girls are more comfortable and free Athletes has built this complex for women. In this complex, sports competitions are held every week between different teams in order to recognize the top figures and participate in higher competitions.
